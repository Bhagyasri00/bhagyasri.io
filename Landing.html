MNIST Handwritten Digit Classification

In this project, Developed an end-to-end pipeline using TensorFlow and Keras for training and evaluating a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) model on the MNIST dataset. Achieved a test accuracy of 91.82%, demonstrating the effectiveness of the model in accurately classifying handwritten digits. Additionally, developed an inference script for seamless deployment and visualized sample predictions to provide insights into the model's performance.

In this project, I analyzed employee turnover data to uncover the main reasons behind attrition rates. Predicting Kyphosis: Age and Vertebrae Analysis

In this project, we employ the Random Forest algorithm to predict the occurrence of kyphosis in patients based on their age and the number of vertebrae involved. Kyphosis, an abnormal spinal curvature, poses challenges in diagnosis and management. By harnessing the power of Random Forest, we aim to enhance our predictive capabilities, offering a valuable tool for healthcare professionals in identifying and addressing kyphosis in patients more accurately.
